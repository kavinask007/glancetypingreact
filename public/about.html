Realistic typing test
Glance typing is designed to test your typing skills by typing the sentence without any onscreen reference as you are typing. This test is a perfect benchmark to find your maximum typing capabilites.

Accuracy

Typing accuracy is defined as the percentage of correct entries out of the total entries typed. Having accuracy less than 90% is not a good typing habit even if your wpm is high.
